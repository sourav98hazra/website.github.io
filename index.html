About Inflatable Packers

Inflatable Packers are used in geotechnical applications (Tunnels,Dams,Mines etc) for high pressure cement injection to make leak proof foundations inside rocks and other unstable terrains.
From Supreme-Pac

Uses of Inflatable Packers(process)

A packer is lowered into the drilled bore-hole. Then the packer is inflated with preferably water through an inflation line. Once the packer is inflated it seals the hole and then pressurized cement is passed for leak proof seal. The concrete fills in the cracks and weak spots for solid impermeable foundation underneath.
